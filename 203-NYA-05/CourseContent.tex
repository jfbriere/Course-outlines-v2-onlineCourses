\entry{Course content}{
The material to be covered is contained in the following chapters and sections of {\bf Physics for Scientists and Engineers by Knight, 4th edition}.
\begin{center}
\begin{tabulary}{0.95\textwidth}{|c|L|L|}
\hline
Weeks & Topics & Chapter \& Section \\ \hline\hline
1 & Concepts of motion & Ch.1: 1--8\\ \hline
2--3 & Kinematics in one dimension & Ch.2: 1--6 (7 optional)\\ \hline
4--5 & Kinematics in two or three dimensions (including circular motion) & Ch.3: 1--4; Ch.4: 1, 2, 4--6\\ \hline
6--7 & Dynamics in one dimension & Ch.5: 1--7; Ch.6: 1--4, 6 (5 optional)\\ \hline
8-9 & Newton's laws & Ch.7: 1--5\\ \hline
10 & Dynamics in two dimensions & Ch.8: 1--5\\ \hline
11 & Work and kinetic energy & Ch. 9: 1-6\\ \hline
12 & Energy	 & Ch.10: 1--4, 7, 8, (5,6 optional)\\ \hline
13 & Impulse and momentum	 & Ch.11: 1--5 (6 optional)\\ \hline
14 & Rotation of a rigid body	& Ch.12: 1, 2, 5--7, 10 (3, 4, 9, 11, 12 optional)\\ \hline
\end{tabulary}
\end{center}
